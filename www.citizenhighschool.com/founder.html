CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE

Chairman's Message

Welcome to Citizen High school!

It is with great pride and enthusiasm that I extend a warm greeting to you. As the Chairman of this esteemed institution, I am honored to be a part of a school that is deeply committed to nurturing the intellectual, emotional, and social growth of its students.

At Citizen High school, we believe in fostering a learning environment that is not only focused on academic excellence but also dedicated to the holistic development of each child. We understand that every student is unique, with individual strengths and potential, and we strive to provide them with the tools and guidance they need to excel in all areas of life. Whether it's through our rigorous curriculum, extracurricular activities, or values-driven teaching, we aim to help students develop into responsible, confident, and compassionate individuals.

Our dedicated staff works tirelessly to create an engaging, innovative, and inclusive learning environment, ensuring that our students are equipped with the skills and knowledge to face the challenges of tomorrow’s world. We also emphasize the importance of ethics, discipline, and character building, which we believe are the cornerstones of a successful and fulfilling life.

I encourage you to explore our website and learn more about the programs and initiatives that make Citizen High school a place where students thrive, both academically and personally. We invite you to be a part of our journey as we continue to grow and strive for excellence.

Thank you for your continued support. Together, let’s build a brighter future for our children.

MR. VADDEPALLY BALA KRISHNA HARI

Chairman, Citizen High school
